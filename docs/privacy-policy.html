Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 31, 2026

Xdosdev LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Rosca App mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email address, phone number (optional), and password
  • Payment Information: Payment method identifiers (such as Zelle email/phone or PayPal email) for facilitating group payments
  • Transaction Data: Records of contributions, payouts, and payment confirmations within savings groups
  • User Content: Payment proof images you upload to verify transactions

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you notifications about group activities and payments

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate rotating savings group (ROSCA) activities
  • Process and track contributions and payouts
  • Enable communication between group members
  • Send push notifications about payment reminders, group updates, and payout schedules
  • Verify payment transactions through uploaded proof images
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and optimize our App
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Group Members: Your name and payment information are shared with other members of savings groups you join to facilitate payments
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate our App:
    • Google Cloud Platform (data hosting and processing)
    • Expo (push notification delivery)
    • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform servers located in the United States. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update: Correct inaccurate information through your profile settings
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Notifications: Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Ad Preferences: Adjust personalized ad settings through your device's privacy settings

7. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google may collect and use data about your device and app usage to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date" above.
