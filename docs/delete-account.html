Rosca App by Xdosdev LLC

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to uobinna@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Rosca App"
  2. Include in your email:
    • The email address associated with your Rosca App account
    • Your full name as registered in the app
    • Confirmation that you want your account permanently deleted
  3. Wait for confirmation. We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
Important: Before requesting deletion, please ensure you have left all active savings groups and have no pending contributions or payouts. Account deletion cannot be reversed.

Data Deletion Details

When your account is deleted, the following data will be affected:

Data Type Action Retention Period
Account information (name, email, phone) Permanently Deleted Immediate
Password and authentication data Permanently Deleted Immediate
Payment method identifiers Permanently Deleted Immediate
Profile settings and preferences Permanently Deleted Immediate
Push notification tokens Permanently Deleted Immediate
Payment proof images Permanently Deleted Within 30 days
Transaction history (contributions, payouts) Anonymized & Retained Up to 7 years*

*Transaction records may be retained in anonymized form for legal, tax, and regulatory compliance purposes. These records will not be linked to your personal identity after account deletion.

What Happens to Group Memberships

  • You will be removed from all savings groups
  • Any pending contributions you owe will still need to be fulfilled before deletion
  • Any pending payouts owed to you should be claimed before deletion
  • Group administrators will be notified of your departure
